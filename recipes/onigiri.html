Onigiri

Desciption

Onigiri is a triangle shaped rice ball, usally filled with a kind of spicy or sweet meat. This dish will be filled with a spicy salmon, a creamy yet savory flavor, topped with furikake gives the dish a salty tasty yet sweet and spicy amazing flavor.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Furikake
  • Salmon
  • Japanes Mayo
  • Unagi
  • Rice Viniger
  • Sugar
  • Hot Saouce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. After rice is cooked, mix with rice viniger and a little sugar.
  3. Let rice cool while making the filling.
  4. Cook the fish.
  5. Break the fish up and add mayo, unagi, and hot saouce.
  6. Now that the rice has cooled, start putting rice on a plate to flatten and put a good scoop of filling.
  7. Do your best to close the onigiri, it might be a little hard.
  8. Shape into triangle.
  9. Top with furikake.
  10. Eat cold or heat them up and enjoy them warm.
