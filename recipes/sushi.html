Sushi

Desciption

Rice covered sushi paper, filled with cucumber and crab. This recipe is simple yet amazing tasting dish. The crab and cucumber pair well with one another.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Nori
  • Cucumber
  • Crab

Steps

  1. Follow instructions to cook the rice
  2. Lay nori flat and lay cooked rice on top
  3. Flip the nori over
  4. Thinly cut cucumber into sticks and lat on top of nori
  5. Do the same with the crab
  6. Lastly roll up the flat nori and enjoy
