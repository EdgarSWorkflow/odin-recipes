Nachos

Desciption

These nachos are loaded with cheese, meat, guacamole, and crema. Biting into these nachos is a world of flavors.

Ingredients

  • Tortia chips
  • Guacamole
  • Ground Meat
  • Beans
  • Crema
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Grab your ground meat and cook it.
  2. Make your cheesse saouce.
  3. Make your beans.
  4. Make the guacamole.
  5. Layer chips onto plate.
  6. Add the Ingredients you made.
  7. Enjoy
